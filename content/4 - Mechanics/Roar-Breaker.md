*Wondrous Item, common (optional attunement)*
> - Can use Strength in place of Charisma when making Intimidation checks.
> On Attunement: 
> - Pick either Sleight of Hand or Persuasion. You now apply your Proficiency Bonus to that skill as a negative, called a Proficiency Malus.
> - Additionally, your Roar-Breaker gains two charges. You may spend one charge to add a d4 bonus to an Intimidation check you make either before or after you roll. You regain all charges on a long rest, but may engage in or observe wrestling of a beast the same size as a Bear or larger before then. When observing or engaging in strength spectacle of that magnitude, you may gain back a charge of the item once in this way before needing a Long Rest to regain that utility aspect.
