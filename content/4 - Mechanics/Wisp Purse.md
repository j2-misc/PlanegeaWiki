*Wondrous Item, common*

The purse has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action to inspect the inside, expend 1 charge, and cast the [Message](<https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:message>) spell. The Purse regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn or one charge when ten minutes of light activity is spent patching part of it with Weaver's Supplies. When patched with Weaver's Supplies, the item can only regain one charge a day when patched like this.
