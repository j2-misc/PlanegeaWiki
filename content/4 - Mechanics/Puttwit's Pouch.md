*Wondrous Item, uncommon*

This pouch has an interior space considerably larger than its outside dimensions, roughly hand width in diameter at the mouth and 2 feet deep. The pouch can hold seemingly limitless items, so long as the items can fit through the mouth of the pouch. Retrieving an item from the pouch requires an action. 

Placing items without solid substance into the bag seemingly fails to capture them, such as water or stew. Food also continues to spoil when in the bag.

If the pouch is pierced or torn, it ruptures and is destroyed, and its contents are scattered outwards from the pouch violently. If the pouch is turned inside out, its contents spill forth, unharmed, but the pouch must be put right before it can be used again. Breathing creatures inside the pouch can survive up to a number of minutes equal to 10 divided by the number of creatures (minimum 1 minute), after which time they begin to suffocate.

Placing a similar item of holding inside an extradimensional space created by a handy haversack, portable hole, or similar item instantly makes something wild and probably bad happen.
