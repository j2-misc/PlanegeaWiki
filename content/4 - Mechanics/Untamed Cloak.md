*Wondrous Item, common*

This cloak has 1 charge. On failing a Strength or Dexterity saving throw, doff the cloak and sent it unmade into tatters, getting to add a 1d6 bonus to the roll, potentially turning a failure into a success.

Regardless of if the save is passed or failed, the cloak is destroyed on use.
