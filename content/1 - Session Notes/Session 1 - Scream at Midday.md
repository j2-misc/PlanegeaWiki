[[Tall Tale]] was a giant mass of fur the color of a dead channel, tall as a tree, in the shape of a tail. In a Columbo-esque voice, "Seelie and Unseelie, disparate and spread, let me tell you a story. I see you all are so close and familiar to me, as to when I told this story first a long, long time ago. There was a plucky girl with a real spitfire of a lion by her side. There was a strange, strange thing got spat up from the [[Venom Abyss]]. There was a streak of white with a lotta bite, lemme tell ya. And last but not least, there was... I'mgonnatellyouastory. It's been a while so I might not remember all the bits and bobs, ends and odds, but I wanna tell all of you about this adventure. We're all fallin', fallin' through time before engines rumbled, before steamships fired canons across oceans, before the time of knights and armor, castles and cauldrons, even before that, before gladiators, before the pyramids, fall through time with me to an old world of fire and ice and old magic. Fall with me into Planegea, the first of all worlds! Here, the volcano that birthed the worlds, and stars wheel overhead in a never-ending dance. All peoples judged by only one law, the law of survival. Will they survive long enough to carve their story into the walls or die in the wilderness? For now though, take a breath and smell smoke. In the early afternoon..."

[[Reokosh]]
- Midday, Venom Moon Clan had been there for one day
- One person was carrying a piece of wood for the [[Clanfire]], around which the strength of the [[Lion Clan]] is established
- People constructing platforms in the trees
- People cooking
- She noticed a big green (like an emerald meets a sponge) dragonborn overseeing things
- They were (surprisingly) older
- [[Yug'zesh]]
- As he went to speak they heard a scream from the distance

[[Bravemeek]]
- Explaining to a young half-elven girl that elves don't come from the stars, they come from peoples' dreams
- [[Glow]], a double sized green monarch butterfly looking thing, fluttered onto Bravemeek's shoulder, "Some of us're from nightmares, actually."
- As a parent approached, he heard a scream in the distance and responded in kind

[[Hafsah]] and [[Kefirah]]
- While lounging about at the edge of camp, they heard a scream from the distance

[[Lonescar]]
- Somewhere else on the clan outskirts, he heard a roar, not the same as where he knew Hafsah and Kefirah were patrolling
- On top of that, there was a scream
- He threw a sound ahead of himself as he approached cautiously

- Bravemeek noticed Reokosh and asked her how she was doing
- She looked at him weird and continued toward the scream
- Everyone convened
- Reokosh smelled a cook fire upwind, not the [[Venom Moon Clan]]'s
- Likely a gatherer was chased down by some [[Ape Clan]]
- Lonescar lagged behind while Hafsah took note of good vantage points
- Reokosh, Bravemeek, and Kafirah pressed forward

- Pressed down a slope, following a branching stream which broke into a miniature [[The Unfalls]]

![[Battlemap 1.png]]

- [[Abzi]] was at the top of the unfall, bound and gagged
- Bravemeek asked, very casually, what happened
- She said they needed to be quiet, and that she was traveling with her life partner, [[Lanleah]], when the Ape Clan found them and chased after them, and she hadn't been able to find her since
- A seemingly imposing lion towered behind her, though Hafsah could tell it was afraid
- While Bravemeek promised to help Abzi and save her partner, Hafsah and Reokosh noticed the sounds of approaching movement
- Reokosh saw past the brush a number of [[Ape Clan]] members readying an ambush
- Despite that Ape Clan was mentioned, many of the attackers wore bear paraphernalia

- Bravemeek teleported with Reokosh who then leapt up to the [[Bear Clan]] charmer, taking a strike from a bola but breaking free in the ensuing flurry of attacks
- A massive berserker charged into the fray and lobbed an axe at Kafirah
- Bravemeek ran forward, catching his hand on an nodeal wave, casting some magic on the charmer, retreating distractingly without the chance of pursuit
- Hafsah and Kafirah then attacked the berserker in tandem, when suddenly Lonescar struck from his hiding spot with a blowgun, killing the berserker, as Lonescar was not beholden to the [[Charm of Sport]]
- Reokosh rent the Charmer brutally unconscious and issued a challenge to the accompanying spearmen, who fled to fight another day
- Bravemeek meanwhile enchanted the skeleton, hindering it, and [[Glow]] (who was not under the [[Charm of Sport]], but a non-murderous soul) poisoned one of the assailants near the berserker rendering them unconscious but not unstably or extraordinarily so
- Hafsah and Kafirah ended the battle with an arrow loosed and a claw fallen upon the last remaining conscious enemies

- Lonescar pocketed [[Puttwit's Pouch]]
- Reokosh looted a badass bearskin cloak, as well as a pouch of arcane components, and a jar with an etching of a mouth which whispered "Bob's Jar" when touched
- She tossed Bob's Jar to Bravemeek. It contained a special kind of paint that responded to vibration, [[Rumbleglow Paint (Bob's Jar)]]
- Lonescar suggested taking the other clansmen prisoner, but Hafsah pointed out they weren't in position to keep prisoners, that they only needed to find more information about [[Abzi]]'s partner
- Abzi noted that she hadn't seen these people when she was first attacked
- Regardless, they picked up the people and began dragging them back to camp

- Lonescar noticed claw marks and mystically called back to the party that they were being hunted, before then hearing a voice, "Abzi?... Abzi?..."
- Abzi began moving quicker toward the sound
- Lonescar again called to the party to group and hide
- Lonescar grazed by something without hearing it
- Only Hafsah saw a strange monster speaking like a parrot, "Abzi... Help..."
- A therizinosaurus, in this world, a [[Great Claw]]
